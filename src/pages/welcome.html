before we start — what jobmeta does with your data

  • your profile lives on your machine. name, resume bullets, skills — stored locally, synced across browsers if you're signed into Chrome/Firefox sync.
  • cover letters are drafted on our server. when you click "generate", we send the job description + your profile to scrb.voiddo.com and stream back a draft. the request is not retained beyond 24 hours.
  • match scores work the same way. we score the fit between you and a job description on the server, return the score + reasoning, and forget the request.
  • we don't sell your data. we don't ship it to advertisers. we don't fingerprint you. read the privacy policy for the details.
